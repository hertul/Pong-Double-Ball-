🏓 Pong (Double Ball) – Multi‑Language Edition
A classic Pong game with a twist – two balls bounce around the arena simultaneously!
Control your paddle, defend your goal, and score points while juggling two balls with different speeds and angles.
Built in 7 programming languages – each implementation features a playable terminal‑based version with AI opponent, scoring, and increasing difficulty.

✨ Features
Two balls – each with independent speed and direction.

Player vs AI – left paddle is player‑controlled; right paddle is AI (or second player).

Controls – W / S (or arrow keys) to move your paddle.

Scoring – earn points when the opponent misses a ball.

Speed increase – ball speed increases slightly after each rally.

Paddle collision – balls bounce off paddles with angle reflection.

Wall bounce – balls bounce off top and bottom walls.

Game over – first to 5 points wins (configurable).

Restart – press R to restart.

Cross‑platform – runs in any terminal (Windows, macOS, Linux).

🎮 Controls
W / ↑ – move paddle up

S / ↓ – move paddle down

R – restart after game over

Q / Esc – quit

📦 Game Mechanics
Arena: 40×20 grid.

Balls: two balls start from the centre with random angles; they move independently.

Paddles: left (player) and right (AI) – each 4 cells tall.

Scoring: +1 point when the opponent misses.

Win condition: first to 5 points.

Speed: ball speed increases by 5% after each hit (capped).
